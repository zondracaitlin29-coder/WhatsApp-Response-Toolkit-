✦ Pro Tips for Better Replies

Always personalise

Replace [Name] with the customer's actual name. A personalised message converts 3x better than a generic one.

Reply within 1 hour

Responding within 60 minutes increases the chance of closing a sale by over 60%. Speed matters.

End with a question

Always close your message with a question to keep the conversation going — "Does that work for you?" keeps things moving.

Don't over-apologise

For complaints, acknowledge once and move to solutions. Over-apologising can make problems seem bigger than they are.
